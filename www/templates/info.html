SARA Info center

Each survey and active task is worth 30 points.

Sunday's survey is longer, but you can earn 50 extra points.

More points mean more fishes in the aquarium

Badges are unlocked when surveys/active tasks are completed on consecutive days

Each badge also comes with some money

Longer streaks give higher
financial incentives

  • • 3-day streak gives 25 cents
  • • 6-day streak gives 50 cents
  • • 12-day streak gives 1 dollars
  • • 18-day streak gives 1.5 dollars
  • • 30-day streak gives 2.5 dollars

Streaks for surveys and active tasks are separate. So, if you complete 3-day streaks on both survey and active tasks then you will get 50 cents.

Sometimes you will get memes after you complete the surveys

Sometimes you will get insights on your collected data after you complete your active tasks
